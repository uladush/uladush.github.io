Privacy Policy

Boxix · Valagame · Last updated: July 5, 2026

This policy explains what data Boxix collects, why, and how it is handled. By playing Boxix you agree to this policy.

1. Data we collect

Always on Firebase Crashlytics

Crash reports and stack traces used to detect and fix stability issues. Collects: app version, device model, OS version, anonymous device identifier, crash call stack. No personal information or gameplay data is included.

Provider: Google — firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Consent-gated Firebase Analytics

App usage events to understand how players interact with the game. Collects: screen views, session duration, device info, anonymous user ID. Disabled by default — enabled only if you accept data collection in the app.

Provider: Google — firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Consent-gated Amplitude Analytics

Gameplay analytics — level completions, session events, menu navigation. Collects: anonymous device ID, event names and properties, session timestamps. No name, email, or account data. Enabled only if you accept data collection.

Provider: Amplitude — amplitude.com/privacy

Ad serving Unity Ads

Used to display interstitial ads in the game. May collect: advertising ID (GAID/IDFA), device info, IP address, ad interaction data (impressions, clicks). Unity Ads uses this data to serve and measure ads.

Provider: Unity Technologies — unity.com/legal/game-player-and-app-user-privacy-policy

2. What we don't collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or payment information. No account or login is required to play Boxix.

3. Data sharing

Data is shared only with the SDK providers listed above (Google, Amplitude, Unity) for the purposes described. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data retention

Crash data is retained for 90 days by Firebase. Analytics data is retained according to each provider's default policy. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. Children's privacy

Boxix is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your rights

You can opt out of analytics collection in the app settings. To request deletion of any collected data, contact us by email and we will process the request within 30 days.
